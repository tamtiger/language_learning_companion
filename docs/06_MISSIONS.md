# Real-world Communication Missions

> Learn English by completing real workplace missions.

The goal of this curriculum is not to memorize conversations.

The goal is to communicate successfully while completing real tasks.

Every mission simulates a real situation that Software Engineers experience.

---

# Learning Philosophy

Traditional English courses teach isolated conversations.

Example

Restaurant

↓

Airport

↓

Hotel

↓

Shopping

Real life is different.

Communication always happens inside a workflow.

This curriculum practices complete workflows instead of isolated dialogues.

---

# Difficulty Levels

Every mission has three levels.

Level 1

Guided

You receive structure and hints.

---

Level 2

Semi-guided

Only objectives are provided.

---

Level 3

Real Simulation

AI acts as coworkers, managers, customers or interviewers.

Nothing is scripted.

---

# Mission Completion

A mission is complete only when you can:

✓ Complete every conversation naturally.

✓ Handle unexpected questions.

✓ Explain your reasoning.

✓ Ask follow-up questions.

✓ Stay in English for the entire mission.

---

# Final Goal

Do not practice English conversations.

Practice completing real work in English.

That is the fastest path to professional fluency.
